## Submission risk checks

- [ ] We do not describe the grant as salaries, runway, or marketing.
- [ ] We do not claim traction we cannot prove.
- [ ] We do not call it a generic MCP wrapper.
- [ ] We explicitly explain why Celo is necessary.
- [ ] We explicitly map to Frontier categories.
- [ ] We include exact Celo mainnet links, not vague "deployed soon" language.
- [ ] We name what the grant unlocks after the live MVP.
- [ ] We do not overclaim x402 hosted facilitator support on Celo.
- [ ] We call the score readiness/conformance, not trust/safety.
- [ ] We explain why this complements 8004scan/AgentScan instead of competing with them.
