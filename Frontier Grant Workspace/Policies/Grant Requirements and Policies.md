# Grant Requirements and Policies

## Confirmed program shape

- Frontier Pool funds AI and agent economy infrastructure on Celo.
- Estimated 4-6 grants.
- Grant size is up to about 25,000 USDm per project.
- Applications close 30 June 2026.
- Rolling review, with outcomes expected within 2-4 weeks.
- One-off grant structure.
- Payment structure: 20% at project onset, 80% after agreed delivery.
- Applicants sign a contract and complete KYC.

## Baseline eligibility

- Working deployment on Celo mainnet.
- Infrastructure focus: tooling, protocols, or services used by other builders or agents.
- Technical credibility, with open-source/public docs preferred.
- Clear ecosystem contribution.
- ERC-8004 registration for agent projects; pure AI infrastructure may be exempt.
- Self Agent ID is requested by the form.
- Verifiable onchain Celo mainnet activity.

## Explicit exclusions

Do not frame budget or scope around:

- ongoing salaries;
- core operating costs;
- general runway;
- pure marketing spend;
- events;
- liquidity provision;
- token listings;
- general VC investment;
- end-user apps without infrastructure value;
- projects not deployed on Celo mainnet;
- thin wrappers over existing tools.

## Practical interpretation

The strongest application is not an idea deck. It is:

- a live narrow MVP;
- verified Celo contracts;
- public GitHub/docs;
- public demo;
- identity/discovery evidence;
- onchain transaction proof;
- a realistic first 10-100 users plan;
- milestone-based budget for hardening and adoption.

## Legal/KYC notes

The form asks for lead applicant, email, company registration country, and grant delivery address. Have these ready before submission. Do not improvise legal/entity details inside the technical draft.
